# Product Guide: openQuote

## 1. Product Vision
openQuote is a streamlined Chromium extension designed to empower Independent Freight Brokers and Logistics Coordinators at 3PLs. Its primary mission is to transform the spot quoting process, reducing the time required to calculate quotes from minutes to seconds through a highly efficient, keyboard-centric interface.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Independent Freight Brokers:** Professionals who need quick, reliable tools to manage their own book of business without enterprise-level overhead.
*   **Logistics Coordinators at 3PLs:** Team members who need to standardize and speed up their quoting workflows in high-volume environments.

## 3. Core Goals
*   **Speed:** Minimize the friction in the quoting process to allow for near-instant calculations.
*   **Efficiency:** Enable a workflow that keeps the user's hands on the keyboard and their focus on the numbers.

## 4. Key Features
*   **Advanced Keybindings:** Implementation of comprehensive keyboard shortcuts for all core actions (calculating, resetting, saving) to eliminate reliance on the mouse.
*   **Smart UI Recognition:** Context-aware commands that recognize the active element or current state of the application to provide relevant options.
*   **Intelligent Suggestions:** A smart recommendation engine that offers suggestions based on user activity and historical inputs.
